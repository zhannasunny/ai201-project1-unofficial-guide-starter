The Bull Runner is USF's free bus service available to all students, faculty, and staff. It connects the main Tampa campus to surrounding off-campus housing complexes including The Standard, HUB Tampa, and nearby neighborhoods. Students without a car can use the Bull Runner to get to and from campus at no cost. Routes run Monday through Thursday 7 AM to midnight, Friday 7 AM to 6 PM, and Saturday/Sunday 11 AM to 6 PM.
